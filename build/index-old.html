PixelArt – Medium is the message

Whether you are a large corporation or coffee house on the corner, PixelArt puts a simple, yet powerful, Digital Signage (DS) Content Management System (CMS) at your disposal. With our CMS you can control and manage your DS from any device

Unlike other CMS, Pixelart allows you to rent out your screens and earn additional revenue


To receive updates form us subscribe to our newsletter

Connect your screen

You can connect various players to PixelArt CMS, both professional media players and consumer devices like Google Chromecast, Amazon Fire TV stick and Intel stick

Create and manage your Campaigns

You can create and distribute your content in just three simple steps:

  • Create a screen layout (split screens) of your choice

  • Upload content (PixelArt CMS supports various media formats: Video, Image, Doc, URL)

  • Create a custom schedule for your content to appear on the screens (chose when and where)

Monitor your network in real time from anywhere

  • Receive live data about what’s playing on your screens and the frequency your content is displayed
  • Measure and optimize your media content to increase the effectiveness of your communication
